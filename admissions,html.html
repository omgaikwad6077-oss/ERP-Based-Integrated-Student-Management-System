Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline for 2025 admissions?

The application deadline for most programs is July 31, 2025. However, we have rolling admissions for some courses. We recommend applying as early as possible as seats are limited.

Is there an entrance exam for all programs?

Most of our technical programs require an entrance assessment, but our business and arts programs have a different evaluation process. Check the specific program page for details.

Can I apply for multiple programs?

Yes, you can apply for up to 3 programs with a single application fee. You'll need to indicate your order of preference during the application process.

What makes Nexus Academy different from other colleges?

Nexus Academy offers AI-powered personalized learning, VR labs, global exposure programs, and a strong focus on industry-relevant skills that set us apart from traditional colleges.
