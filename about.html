About Nexus Academy

Innovating Education
for the Digital Age

Founded in 2010, Nexus Academy was born from a simple idea: education should evolve with technology. What started as a small tutorial center has grown into India's most technologically advanced educational institution, serving over 5,000 students annually.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional education and the skills needed for the 21st century. We believe in learning that's interactive, personalized, and relevant to the real world.

Our Journey

2010

Nexus Academy founded as a tutorial center in Bangalore with 20 students.

2012

Launched first online course platform with 100+ video lectures.

2015

Opened first full-fledged campus with smart classrooms and VR labs.

2018

Partnered with MIT for curriculum development and faculty exchange.

2020

Introduced AI-powered personalized learning paths for all students.

2023

Launched India's first fully hybrid degree programs.

2025

Expanded to 5 campuses across India with 5,000+ students.
